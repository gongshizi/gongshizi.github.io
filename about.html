About UA Technology

We specialise in television repair, refurbishment of factory returns, wholesale supply and spare parts. Based in Melbourne, we are committed to quality service and honest pricing for both individual customers and business partners.

Learn more

Our Story

UA Technology was founded to meet the growing need for professional, reliable TV repair services. With over 8 years of experience, we have partnered with major brands including TCL to refurbish returned and factory-second televisions, giving them a second life and reducing waste. Our workshop in Melbourne handles diagnostics, repairs, and wholesale supply.

Growth & Services

From a small repair shop, we have grown to handle large volumes of televisions for refurbishment and wholesale. Today we not only repair and refurbish TVs, but also supply tested spare parts, provide on-site service, and support local retailers with bulk orders. Our focus is on long-term reliability, customer satisfaction, and sustainable electronics use.
