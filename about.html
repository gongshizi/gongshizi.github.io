About UA Technology

We specialise in television repair, refurbishment of factory returns, wholesale supply and spare parts. Based in Melbourne, we deliver reliable service and honest pricing for individuals and business partners.

Learn more

Our Story

Founded to meet the growing need for professional TV repair, we’ve partnered with major brands to refurbish factory returns and reduce e‑waste. Our Melbourne workshop handles diagnostics, repairs, QA and wholesale supply.

Growth & Services

From a small repair shop to handling large refurbishment volumes—today we repair and refurbish TVs, supply tested parts, provide on‑site service, and support retailers with bulk orders and warranties.
